# 🌿 Haven

## Your ambient wellness companion — track habits, log moods, breathe, and grow a digital garden, all in one calm space.

---

### 💡 The Idea
Most productivity apps feel like dry checklists that add stress instead of removing it. Haven flips that: it merges habit tracking with mental wellness in a calming, nature-inspired interface where every completed habit grows a living garden, every mood reshapes the theme, and every breath brings you back to center. Productivity that actually feels good.

---

### ✨ Features

- **🌱 Habit Garden** — Track micro-habits with a gamified SVG garden that grows leaves and flowers as you complete tasks.
- **🎨 Mood Matrix** — Log how you feel (Radiant, Focused, Tired, Anxious, Calm) and watch the app's theme adapt to your headspace.
- **🫁 Animated Breathing Guide** — Guided box-breathing sessions with fluid SVG animations and a 1-minute progress ring.
- **💧 Hydration Tracker** — Tap to log water intake; an SVG glass fills up beautifully with each glass.
- **📅 Progress Calendar** — Visualize your habits, moods, and hydration day by day across the month.
- **📊 Weekly Insights** — Streak badges, daily summaries, and clean SVG charts showing your wellness trends.
- **🌌 Premium Ambient UI** — Forest-green glassmorphic design, organic micro-interactions, and 60 FPS animations.
- **🔒 100% Local & Private** — Client-side only. Your data lives in your browser via LocalStorage. Zero accounts, zero tracking.

---

### 🎯 Mission
To turn habit tracking into a moment of calm rather than a source of pressure — helping people build consistent, healthy routines through ambient design, gentle gamification, and mindful pauses, so wellness becomes something they *return to*, not something they *push through*.

---

### 🚀 Call to Action
**Open `index.html` and start growing your garden today.**
No sign-up. No setup. Just plant your first habit, take one deep breath, and let Haven hold space for the version of you that's quietly thriving.
