The Idea

Most productivity apps feel like dry checklists that add stress instead of removing it. Haven flips that — merging habit tracking with mental wellness in a nature-inspired interface where every completed habit grows a living garden, every mood reshapes the theme, and every breath brings you back to center.

Productivity that actually feels good.

Mission

Turn habit tracking into a moment of calm — so wellness becomes something you return to, not something you push through.
